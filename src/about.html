History

Amaze Manufacturing Company is a leading Manufacturers exporter of Copper Bath Tubs, Wash Basins & Kitchen Sinks (Copper & Stain less steel). Amaze Manufacturing Company, established in 2003, it has been only over the last 16 years that we have experienced the tremendous change that has brought us to where we are today.

Amaze Manufacturing Company, maintains strong working ethics and relationships with its Clients. This gives the company control over the products it handles, and the ability to react quickly and efficiently to customers’ changing needs.

Team

Amaze Manufacturing Company is a proprietor ship concern. Mr. Riyaz Anwer is a founder and mentor. He also supervises the manufacturing & R & D section. He deals with every day challenges of production manufacturing technique. Mr. Riyaz Anwer is considered the only man to introduce Copper Bathtubs & Kitchen Sinks in Moradabad. He is technically expert.

Mr. Riyaz Anwer has an extensive background in the Copper bath tubs & Kitchen sinks etc. He has a keen eye for design and practical knowledge. Riyaz Anwer has inherited this manufacturing skill and ability from his ancestors who have been recognized as peerless leaders in their field for many generations. 150 artisans work under his direct supervision. The beauty of our company lies in the unique expression of each item. Our designers have great expertise in their skill and they put their heart into every item created.

Fabrication Facility

Amaze Manufacturing Company produces quality copper and manufactures copper products in it’s own fabrication facility. Most sink suppliers purchase their products from a third party, having no control over the manufacturing process. We have a covered area of 25000 Sq. FT. We use only durable & heavier sheets in making our product. Normally, we use copper sheets in 16 gauges with copper contents 99.95%. The copper we use is only the finest of solid copper and our sinks are completely food-safe i.e. we also provide Lab testing report verifying the genuineness of copper.

Our Support

Amaze Manufacturing Company produces sinks/tubs with European Standards in place. The sinks are specifically engineered to work with Standardized European plumbing. Our entire line of products has been developed with you in mind. We support our customers with quality products, Timely shipment and reasonable rates. We develop products as per your confidential designs, specifications and requirements with our well-equipped manufacturing unit.

Finishes

We make our product in different finishes as; Shinny polished, Copper Antique, Patinas- Old French patina, Nickel, Pewter and Tin-plated.

Quality Control

We do not compromise on quality because it is the prime factor. To save our customers from receiving poor quality or poor specification goods, we maintain strict quality control during manufacturing of our products. Every item is carefully inspected before it is shipped. Therefore, our buyers receive the quality that they need. We take the responsibility for damaged or breakage of the goods in the form of replacement.

On line Inspection

We provide on line inspection on the items that we pack.

Packing

Our packing is very exclusive having the norms of UPS packaging.

We use polythene bags + bubbler + PU Foam. Thence we put it into strong plywood case. We pack the Tubs/Sinks inside the cases in such a manner that movement inside the cases is impossible. The inside of case is “lined” with various materials e.g. stout & damp resisting paper etc. to prevent the damage by water, air, or insects. In addition, the goods is wrapped & generously padded to avoid scratching and knocking against the cases.
